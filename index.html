3AM Digital Ministry

"Guiding the Curious, Strengthening the Believers"

Spreading the gospel in the digital space through prayer, Bible study, and discipleship

The 3AM-Digital Ministry is an evangelistic and missionary platform designed to reach:

  • Believers seeking deeper biblical understanding
  • Agnostics, atheists, and non-believers exploring faith
  • Individuals searching for spiritual guidance ("fumbling in the dark")

We operate as an integrated platform for prayer, Bible study, discipleship, and media resources centered on the Three Angels' Message (Revelation 14:6-12).

Our Constant Prayer:

Draw them from shadow to Your love's desire.
One heart transformed, one life made whole,
A disciple true for Your Kingdom's goal."

Proposed Responsibility Structure

Each team member will take primary responsibility for 2 days per week:

  • Team Member 1: Sunday & Wednesday
  • Team Member 2: Monday & Thursday
  • Team Member 3: Tuesday & Friday
  • Team Member 4: Support role across all days + content planning

Content Management

  • Create a content calendar 2-4 weeks in advance
  • Develop "carry forward" content for emergencies or unexpected impediments
  • Establish a shared cloud folder for storing and organizing ministry materials
  • Weekly team check-in via Zoom (30 minutes) to coordinate activities

Content Creation Standards

  1. Biblical Accuracy: All content must be biblically sound and aligned with the Three Angels' Message.
  2. Accessibility: Content should be accessible to both believers and non-believers.
  3. Quality: Maintain high standards for all media (clear audio, high-resolution images, error-free text).
  4. Consistency: Use consistent branding, tone, and messaging across all platforms.
  5. Relevance: Address contemporary issues through a biblical lens.

Post Structure Guidelines

  • Motivational/Inspiring Posts:
    • Start with a compelling Bible verse or quote
    • Include a brief (2-3 paragraph) reflection
    • End with a practical application or question
    • Add relevant image with ministry branding
  • Bible Studies:
    • Clear topic introduction
    • 3-5 key Bible passages with explanations
    • Discussion questions
    • Summary of main points
    • Call to action or application
  • Prayer Sessions:
    • Theme for the prayer session
    • Opening scripture reading
    • Guided prayer points
    • Time for personal requests
    • Closing prayer

Platform-Specific Guidelines

  • TikTok: 30-60 second videos with clear, concise message
  • Instagram: High-quality images with thoughtful captions
  • Facebook: More detailed posts with opportunity for discussion
  • YouTube: Longer-form content like Bible studies and sermons
  • WhatsApp: Daily devotionals and announcements
  • TikTok evangelism series with local field connections
  • Expert guest speakers for special topics
  • Seasonal online events (Bible studies, prayer vigils)
  • Collaborative projects with other ministries
